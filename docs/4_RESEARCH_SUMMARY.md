## Category-Specific Findings (Verified Data)

### Travel (Airlines + Hotels)
**Leader:** FAB Travel Card (12% verified March 28)

**Unique advantage:** Zero forex markup (critical finding)
- FAB Travel Card: 0% forex on all spending
- All other USD/international cards: 2.3–3.5% forex
- **Impact:** On AED 20K international spend, FAB Travel saves AED 460–700 in forex fees alone

**Reward structure verified:**
- 41 FAB Rewards/AED airlines + hotels
- Earns at base rates on other spend (0.9% domestic, 0% intl except flights/hotels)
- Requires AED 5K previous month spend threshold
- Cap: AED 1,800/month combined

**Key exclusion:** No international rewards outside airlines & hotels (verified in T&C)

**Recommendation (from verified data):** FAB Travel Card is unmatched for international travelers. No other verified card combines 12% earn + 0% forex.

## Effective Return % Reality Check (Verified Data)

**FAB Point Value:** 0.003 AED/point (verified across all FAB cards)

This means effective returns are much lower than advertised earn rates:

| Card | Advertised | Points/AED | Point Value | Effective % |
|------|-----------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| FAB Travel (airlines) | 12% | 41 points | 0.003 | **12.3%** |
| FAB Elite (brands) | 6% | 20 points | 0.003 | **6%** |
| FAB Cashback | 5% | N/A | Cashback | **5%** |
| FAB Rewards Indulge | 1.5% | 5 points | 0.003 | **1.5%** |

**Key insight:** FAB points-based cards need high point values or transfer options to be competitive. Cashback cards are transparent and straightforward.
